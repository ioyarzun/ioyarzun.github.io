About me

My name is Iñigo Oyarzun. I am a Bioinformatician under construction looking for professional challenges where I can use and keep improving my analysis and programming skills that I developed during my MSc in Bioinformatics for Health Sciences and internships. Born in Pamplona/Iruña and raised in Barañain, a small town that everybody considers a neighborhood of Pamplona, I studied at the High School I.E.S. of Barañain. Here is where I started to be interested in science thanks to some great teachers I had. That's why I decided to study Biology in the University of Salamanca, which is, by the way, the perfect Universitary city. Biology is, in my opinion, the most beautiful degree to study because you learn how life and nature works at all their levels, from a single molecule to a whole ecosystem and that made me change the way I look at the world. Since the first lessons I had in Biostatistics on my Biology bachelor degree I was amazed about how much information is hidden behind all kinds of data and how important it is to find a reliable way to get this information. That is why I decided to enroll in a Bioinformatics Master degree. Due to my background in Biology I was afraid of the “coding world”, but here I started programming and even working with Big Data and, honestly, I have enjoyed it so much. It has been a life changing experience to see the infinitude of possibilities that programming and data science worlds offer.
